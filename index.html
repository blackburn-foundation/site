Our family has always held education as a top priority. Thus, we formed The Blackburn Foundation to promote higher education and community development. Your support of the foundation is an investment in your community. To date we have supported scholarship programs, community development programs, teacher appreciation programs, and (students' favorite) the District 5 Academic Pep Rally.

We encourage you to join us in our support of the community. This can be as simple as making a donation or a bit more active in the form of volunteering.
